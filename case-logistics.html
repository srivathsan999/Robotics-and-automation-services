Project Overview

A major logistics and distribution company needed to modernize their warehouse operations to handle increasing order volumes while maintaining accuracy and reducing costs. The client was struggling with manual picking processes, slow order fulfillment, and high error rates.

We implemented a comprehensive smart warehouse solution combining autonomous mobile robots (AMRs), automated sortation systems, and intelligent warehouse management integration to transform their operations.

The Challenge

The client faced several critical challenges:

  • Slow order fulfillment times unable to meet customer expectations
  • High error rates in manual picking and sorting operations
  • Rising labor costs and difficulty scaling workforce
  • Limited real-time visibility into inventory and order status
  • Inefficient use of warehouse space and resources

Our Solution

We deployed an integrated warehouse automation system:

  • Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMRs): Fleet of intelligent robots for efficient goods-to-person picking
  • Automated Sortation: High-speed sorting systems for order routing and consolidation
  • WMS Integration: Seamless connection with warehouse management systems for real-time tracking
  • RFID Technology: Complete inventory visibility and traceability
  • Machine Learning Routing: AI-powered optimization for pick paths and robot coordination

Results & Impact

The solution delivered exceptional results:

  • 3× Faster Processing: Order fulfillment time reduced by 67%
  • 99.9% Accuracy: Near-perfect order accuracy eliminating costly errors
  • 50% Labor Cost Reduction: Significant savings through automation
  • Scalable Operations: System handles peak season volumes effortlessly
  • Real-Time Visibility: Complete transparency into inventory and order status
  • Improved Space Utilization: Optimized warehouse layout and storage efficiency
